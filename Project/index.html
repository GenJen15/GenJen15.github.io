About Pokémon Generations VI & VII

Explore the evolution of Pokémon games through the Kalos and Alola regions, introducing new mechanics, stunning graphics, and beloved Pokémon!

Generation 6: Kalos Region (2013)

Games: Pokémon X and Pokémon Y
Platform: Nintendo 3DS
Region: Kalos (inspired by France)

  • First fully 3D main series Pokémon game.
  • Introduced Mega Evolution and the Fairy-type.
  • Trainer outfit customization for the first time.
  • Starters: Chespin, Fennekin, Froakie.
  • Legendaries: Xerneas, Yveltal, Zygarde.
Focus Pokémon: Greninja

Greninja became a fan favorite, even receiving its own special form — Ash-Greninja — in the anime!

Generation 7: Alola Region (2016–2017)

Games: Pokémon Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun, Ultra Moon
Platform: Nintendo 3DS
Region: Alola (inspired by Hawaii)

  • Replaced traditional Gyms with Island Trials.
  • Introduced Alolan forms and Z-Moves.
  • Expanded world with Ultra Beasts and alternate dimensions.
  • Starters: Rowlet, Litten, Popplio.
  • Legendaries: Solgaleo, Lunala, Necrozma.
Main Theme: Lunala

Lunala embodies the moon’s power and mystery — a central figure in Pokémon Moon and the Ultra Space storyline.

Fun Facts
  • First major 3D leap for the franchise.
  • Kalos represents fashion & culture, Alola represents nature & diversity.
  • Introduced online features & unique regional mechanics.
